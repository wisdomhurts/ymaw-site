About YMAW

It takes a village.

YMAW was born from a simple belief: every teenage boy deserves a weekend that helps him see himself — and the world — differently.

Our Mission

Transforming lives through adventure and mentorship.

The Young Men's Adventure Weekend offers teenage boys a unique opportunity to develop leadership skills, learn practical outdoor skills, and challenge themselves in a supportive and inclusive environment. We believe this program has the power to transform the lives of the boys who participate.

Through hands-on, experiential learning, YMAW creates a space where young men disconnect from screens and distractions and reconnect with nature, each other, and themselves.

What We Believe

Our Values.

01

Safety First

Safety is our top priority. Every activity is supervised by trained leaders, and all programming is age-appropriate and carefully planned.

02

Inclusivity

YMAW welcomes boys of all backgrounds, fitness levels, and experience. Every participant is supported to learn and grow at their own pace.

03

Experiential Learning

We believe young men learn best by doing. Our program is hands-on, challenging, and designed to build real confidence through real accomplishment.

04

Community

YMAW is powered by a community of dedicated volunteer men who show up to positively influence the next generation. It truly takes a village.

Our Approach

Trained leaders. Proven methods.

Our production team consists of trained leaders skilled in facilitating group dynamics. Activities are designed to be challenging and engaging while ensuring every participant feels safe, supported, and seen.

We foster teamwork and collaboration through structured group challenges, guided reflection, and shared adventure. The bonds formed over the weekend often become some of the most meaningful relationships in a young man's life.

Connected to a larger movement.

YMAW is proud to be connected with organizations that share our commitment to developing young men, including the ManKind Project, Boys to Men, Youth Passageways, Ever Forward Club, and The Kingmakers.

Together, we are part of a global movement dedicated to guiding boys through the critical transition from adolescence to healthy, responsible manhood.
