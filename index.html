About the Weekend

Built for young men who want more than ordinary.

YMAW is an outdoor adventure weekend designed to push limits, build character, and forge lasting friendships. Set in the rugged backcountry of British Columbia, participants take on everything from mountain hikes and river crossings to campfire conversations and team challenges.

No screens. No distractions. Just you, the crew, and the wild.

The Weekend

What's waiting for you.

01

Hiking & Trekking

Backcountry trails through old-growth forest. Guided routes for all skill levels with stunning alpine views.

02

Rock Climbing

Outdoor sport climbing and bouldering with certified guides. Gear provided — just bring your nerve.

03

River Rafting

Class II–III rapids with experienced river guides. Teamwork under pressure — the kind you can't fake.

04

Campfire & Community

Evenings around the fire. Stories, music, conversation — the moments that turn strangers into brothers.

Our Belief

"Every young man deserves a weekend that changes the way he sees himself — and the world around him."

YMAW was founded on the idea that adventure builds character. We create the space — you write the story.
