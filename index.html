About YMAW

A unique opportunity to grow, lead, and discover.

YMAW offers teenage boys a unique opportunity to develop leadership skills, learn practical outdoor skills, and challenge themselves in a supportive and inclusive environment. Through hands-on, experiential learning, participants build confidence, forge lasting friendships, and gain a real sense of accomplishment.

No screens. No distractions. Just challenge, nature, and brotherhood.

The Weekend

Challenging, engaging, unforgettable.

01

Hiking & Orienteering

Backcountry trails and navigation challenges. Learn to read terrain, use a compass, and find your way through old-growth forest.

02

Leadership & Teamwork

Group challenges designed to build trust, communication, and collaboration. Push your limits and discover what you're capable of as a team.

03

Camping & Outdoor Skills

Hands-on learning: shelter building, fire craft, wilderness safety, and practical skills that last a lifetime. All levels welcome.

04

Problem Solving

Real-world challenges that demand creativity, grit, and cooperation. Activities designed so every participant can learn and grow at their own pace.

Frequently Asked Questions

For Parents.

Is my son safe at YMAW?

Safety is our top priority. All activities are supervised by trained leaders skilled in facilitating group dynamics. Activities are age-appropriate and designed to challenge without putting participants at risk.

What ages is YMAW for?

YMAW is designed for teenage boys ages 12–17. Activities are structured so participants can learn and grow at their own pace, regardless of fitness level or outdoor experience.

Is financial assistance available?

Yes. We believe every young man should have the opportunity to attend. Scholarships and financial assistance are available for families in need. Contact us for details.

What should my son bring?

A detailed packing list is provided upon registration. Generally: weather-appropriate clothing, a sleeping bag, personal items, and a willingness to try something new. All activity gear is provided.
